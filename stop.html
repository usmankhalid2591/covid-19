Stop COVID-19
CORONA VIRUS

The App is designed to give you an insight about the novel Coronavirus COVID-19. It will also help you analyze your health by taking a screening test considering all the possible risk factors and advice you accordingly.

Download APK

BACKGROUND LOCATION TRACKING

Stop COVID app anonymously collects location data to enable up-to-date tracking of COVID-19 spread and to show the safety of different places, even when the app is closed or not in use.

Self Assessment

Analyze your health by answering simple questions regarding your current health. As per your answers you could ve either not-suspected, suspected or confirmed case of COVID 19, afterwards you will be provided with the advice that best suits your current health condition

Feel free to Explore.

Safe Places

You will be facilitated by getting to know whether the place you wish to visit is safe from the Virus and you could visit, or it's not and it's better for you to refrain going to the desired location in order to protect yourself from coming into contact with the deadly virus and affecting many others thereupon.

How is a place declared unsafe?

Places are marked unsafe if suspected or confirmed cases have been in the vicinity or bounds of your specified destination.
Therefore it is highly sugeested that you refrain from going to the place marked unsafe.

User Anonymity

We respect your right to privacy!

The end-goal for this application is not to reveal your identity but just keep you and your fellow citizens safe from coming in contact to the deadly Coronavirus COVID-19. In order to collect the facts and figures to form an inference about the expected spread and/or to notify you or many others about the hotspots, we just require your age-range and gender(only if you want to specify) and request you to allow us to monitor your whereabouts.
